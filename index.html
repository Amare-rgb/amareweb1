Welcom to Gondar City

Gondar is a city in the Amhara Region of northern Ethiopia, historically known as a capital of the Ethiopian Empire, and is famous for its royal castles, religious sites, and as a major tourist and commercial hub.

Best place in Gondar City

The best place in Gondar is Fasil Ghebbi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring a royal enclosure of castles and palaces from the 17th and 18th centuries. Other top attractions include the Debre Birhan Selassie Church, with its elaborate murals and faces on the ceiling, and the historic Fasil's Pool.
